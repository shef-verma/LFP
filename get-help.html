Food Distribution

We provide fresh groceries every Saturday morning from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM at our main location:

Lexington Food Pantry

123 Main Street
Lexington, MA 02420

Behind the First Baptist Church

What to Expect

Our market-style distribution allows you to select the items your family needs most. A typical food package includes:

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Protein sources (meat, eggs, or beans)
  • Dairy products
  • Bread and bakery items
  • Pantry staples (rice, pasta, cereal)

Eligibility

We serve all Lexington residents in need. No proof of income is required - we operate on an honor system. Just bring:

  • Photo ID showing Lexington address (or recent utility bill)
  • Reusable bags if you have them

Emergency Food Needs?

If you need immediate assistance outside our regular hours, please call our hotline: (781) 555-HELP
