What We Do

We are a nonprofit organization providing groceries tailored to the individual dietary needs of each one of our clients.

Who we Are

We are a 501(c)3 non-profit organization staffed entirely by Lexington volunteers. We provide our clients with fresh produce, pantry staples, and household goods per client requests.

Location and Hours

We are located at the Church of Our Redeemer: 6 Meriam St, Lexington, MA.

Every Saturday, from 9:30 - Noon (Excluding Holidays)
