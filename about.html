About Us

Our story, mission, and vision for Lexington

Our History

From humble beginnings to community cornerstone

1982

Pantry Founded

Started in a church basement serving 15 families weekly

1995

First Permanent Home

Moved to our current location with dedicated refrigeration

2010

Home Delivery Launched

Began serving homebound seniors with weekly deliveries

2020

Pandemic Response

Served triple our typical families during COVID crisis

Our Mission

To alleviate hunger in Lexington by providing nutritious food with dignity, while building community connections and advocating for systemic change.

Core Values

  • Dignity

    Everyone deserves respect when accessing food assistance

  • Sustainability

    We prioritize food rescue and waste reduction

  • Community

    Neighbors helping neighbors is at our core
