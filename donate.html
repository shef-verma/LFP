Your Gift Makes a Difference

Every dollar you donate helps us provide fresh, nutritious food to our neighbors facing food insecurity. With your support, we can continue to serve over 1,200 families each month with dignity and compassion.

$25 provides a family with fresh produce for a week
$100 stocks our pantry with essential staples for a day
$500 supports our home delivery program for seniors

Lexington Food Pantry is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.
