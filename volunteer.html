Volunteer FAQs

Do I need experience?

No! We provide training for all positions. Just bring your willingness to help.

Can I volunteer with my family?

Yes! We welcome volunteers ages 12+ (with adult supervision for those under 16).

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes. We provide aprons and gloves.
