How We Operate

The Lexington Food Pantry is a completely volunteer-run organization serving our community with care and efficiency.

About the Lexington Food Pantry

The Lexington Food Pantry is a completely volunteer-run organization operating out of the Church of Our Redeemer at 6 Meriam St. in Lexington, Massachusetts. We utilize both the church's basement and ground floor to prepare and distribute groceries to our community.

Our Workflow

Our volunteers work behind the scenes to ensure every order is carefully prepared and delivered so you receive the groceries you need.

  • Walk-In Orders: When you arrive and provide your menu to the coordinator, your name and order are recorded and sent to our basement team. This team gathers all requested fresh produce, groceries, and household items, carefully packaging them into a cart. The order is then passed to our ground team, made up entirely of dedicated Lexington High School volunteers, who either hand-deliver your order directly to you or place it in your car for easy pickup.
  • Online Orders: Online orders follow a similar process—except when you arrive, your order is already prepared and ready for pickup on our ground floor.

Depending on the number of orders and volunteers on a given day, this process typically takes 15–45 minutes. On average, we prepare and distribute over 1,000 bags of groceries each week, serving more than 200 families!

Our Policies

Closures

The LFP is closed for all major holidays. Please check our online ordering form each week for updates. One week prior to any planned closure, we provide gift cards with a monetary value proportional to your family size to ensure your grocery needs are met during that week.

Extreme Weather

Menus and order sheets are typically located outside the food pantry doors. During rain or snow, all menus will be kept inside with the coordinator and provided upon request. We kindly ask that clients remain in their cars if possible, as volunteers need the ground floor space to move groceries and carts between the parking lot and basement. If necessary, a side room will be opened for clients to wait while their orders are prepared.
