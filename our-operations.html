How We Operate

The Lexington Food Pantry is a completely volunteer-run organization serving our community with care and efficiency.

About the Lexington Food Pantry

We operate out of the Church of Our Redeemer at 6 Meriam St. in Lexington, Massachusetts, utilizing both the church's basement and ground floor to prepare and distribute groceries.

Walk-In Service

Our volunteers work behind the scenes to ensure every order is carefully prepared and delivered so you receive the groceries you need.

When you arrive, your name and order are recorded and sent to our basement team
Our team gathers all requested items, carefully packaging them
Lexington High School volunteers deliver your order to you or your car

Online Orders

For your convenience, we offer online ordering with quick pickup service.

Place your order online anytime during the week
Your order is prepared in advance by our volunteers

Our Workflow

Our efficient process ensures you receive your groceries with care and respect.

1

Order Placement

Clients place orders either in person or online, selecting from our available fresh produce, groceries, and household items.

2

Order Preparation

Our basement team carefully gathers all requested items, ensuring quality and quantity match the client's needs.

3

Order Transfer

Completed orders are passed to our ground floor team, made up of dedicated Lexington High School volunteers.

4

Order Delivery

Volunteers either hand-deliver orders directly to clients or place them in cars for easy pickup.

Closures & Extreme Weather

We want to ensure you're prepared for any changes to our normal operations.

Closed for all major holidays (check our online form for updates)
Before closures, we provide gift cards proportional to family size
During extreme weather, menus are kept inside and clients are asked to wait in cars when possible
